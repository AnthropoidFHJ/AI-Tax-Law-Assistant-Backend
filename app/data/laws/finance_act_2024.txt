FINANCE ACT 2024 - BANGLADESH TAX UPDATES
=========================================

SECTION 1: Tax Rate Updates for Assessment Year 2024-25

For Individual Taxpayers:
- Basic exemption increased to BDT 350,000 (from BDT 300,000)
- Women taxpayers: Basic exemption BDT 375,000
- Senior citizens (65+ years): Basic exemption BDT 475,000
- Freedom fighters: Basic exemption BDT 525,000

TAX SLABS (Assessment Year 2024-25):

General Individual:
- Up to BDT 350,000: 0%
- BDT 350,001 to 450,000: 5%
- BDT 450,001 to 750,000: 10%
- BDT 750,001 to 1,150,000: 15%
- BDT 1,150,001 to 1,650,000: 20%
- Above BDT 1,650,000: 25%

SECTION 2: Investment Rebate Updates

Maximum investment for rebate: BDT 15,00,000
Rebate rate: 15% of eligible investment
Investment options:
- Savings certificates and bonds
- Approved securities
- Life insurance premium
- Provident fund contribution
- Deposit Pension Scheme (DPS)
- Unit certificates

SECTION 3: Digital Service Tax

- 15% tax on digital services provided by non-residents
- Applicable to online advertising, digital content, cloud services

SECTION 4: Advance Tax Requirements

Mandatory advance tax payment for:
- Income above BDT 5,00,000: minimum 10% of estimated tax
- Payment due by 15th December of income year

SECTION 5: Penalty Updates

Late submission penalty:
- Individual: BDT 5,000 or 2% of tax payable (whichever is higher)
- Company: BDT 25,000 or 2% of tax payable (whichever is higher)

Source withholding tax rates:
- Bank interest: 20% for individual
- Dividend: 20% (final settlement for individuals)
